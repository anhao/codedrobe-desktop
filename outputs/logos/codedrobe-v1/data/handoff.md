# CodeDrobe logo handoff

## Selected direction

**Modular C**, concept-board item 4.

Seven rounded theme modules form a compact letter C. The modular construction represents theme packs and creators; the open right side signals switching and extensibility. Violet marks the active theme endpoint, while gold adds a premium creator-market accent.

## Production rules

- Use the full-color mark on white or very light backgrounds.
- Use the light app-icon treatment on the dark application tile.
- Use the monochrome mark when color is unavailable.
- Keep clear space equal to one module thickness around the mark.
- Do not use the mark below 16px; at 16-24px, preserve the original seven segments and gaps.
- Do not add outlines, drop shadows, clothing imagery, or extra colors.

## Files

- `logo-mark.svg`: primary transparent full-color mark.
- `logo-mark-mono.svg`: monochrome mark.
- `logo-app-icon.svg`: dark application-icon treatment.
- `logo-lockup.svg`: provisional horizontal lockup; its live text must be converted to approved outlines before a final trademark package.
